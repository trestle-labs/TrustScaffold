## Cross-Cutting Questions

- Does the baseline set distinguish policy, procedure, system description, and evidence-request content clearly enough?
- Are CC1-CC5 mapped clearly enough to COSO principles for a SOC 2 reviewer?
- Are CC6-CC9 controls specific enough for a SaaS/cloud environment without becoming company-specific?
- Are optional TSCs for Availability, Confidentiality, Processing Integrity, and Privacy sufficiently covered when selected?
- Are placeholders clear and reviewable before company-specific data is inserted?
- Are there claims that should be softened unless backed by evidence during company-specific generation?
- Are any templates missing expected audit evidence, ownership, cadence, exception handling, or review requirements?
